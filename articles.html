Crystals are located at Future (space) Time Locations. During Kronos Phase 4 (Crystal Light), crystals at HQ give a flat +1 to the timeline (+2 if empowered). Crystals in the field at their correct Time Location roll 2d6 vs 9 — success gives +1. Crystals carried or at wrong locations contribute nothing. At setup, each crystal is secretly paired with one artifact — a TimeLord can Empower a crystal at HQ to boost it, earn +1 Kairos, and nerf its paired artifact for the round (skips empower & Timeline Control phases).

Artifacts are fixed to their printed hex locations and never move. An agent at the artifact's hex can Guard it — earning Kairos each round they remain there and making Kronos far less likely to steal it in Phase 1. Kronos targets all artifacts, rolling 2d6 per artifact and stealing on 9+ after modifiers (−5 non-portal, +3 ring-1, +2 Hard / −2 Easy, plus crystal excess and timeline tier, −4 Protected / −2 Guarded). If Kronos holds an artifact, it generates Timeline Control damage every round in Phase 3. Artifacts do not remove blots when recovered. Each artifact is paired with one crystal at setup — empowering that crystal nerfs the artifact for the round.
