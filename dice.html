The destination always contributes a Ring, Base and Planet die; the Direction die is added only when the trip crosses eras. Portal, Adjacent, TimeLord and Baal dice join the pool conditionally. The player rolls up to four times, locking dice between rolls. Wilds substitute for any required value — two skulls means the trip fails.

Each article lists the recovery factors it requires (Archaeology, Security, Cyber, Psychic, Medical, Historian). Colour dice are selected so the pool can cover every required factor, with TimeLord, Baal and Alpha bonus dice added when applicable. The player rolls up to four times and locks dice between rolls; wilds substitute for any factor.
